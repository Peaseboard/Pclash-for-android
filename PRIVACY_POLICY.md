# Privacy Policy - Pclash

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Information Collection

Pclash does not collect any personal information. All profile data and settings are stored locally on your device.

## Network Access

Pclash requires network access to:
- Download proxy profiles from URLs you provide
- Connect to proxy servers configured in your profiles

We do not transmit any data to our own servers.

## Permissions

- **Network**: Required for proxy functionality
- **VPN**: Required for TUN mode traffic routing

## Open Source

Pclash is fully open source. You can review the code at:
https://github.com/Peaseboard/Pclash-for-android
